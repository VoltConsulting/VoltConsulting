Why VoltConsulting in Switzerland

A Market That Demands
True Specialism.

Switzerland is home to some of the world's most significant pharmaceutical and biopharma organisations. It is also one of the most competitive and nuanced talent markets in the world. Generic search approaches do not work here. The talent pools are limited, the roles are highly specialised and the candidates worth speaking to are rarely actively looking.

VoltConsulting has spent over two decades building genuine relationships with senior scientists, functional heads and leadership talent across the Swiss market. We know the landscape, we know the people and we know how to have conversations that lead somewhere meaningful.

Every search we take on in Switzerland is led personally by Brinda Patel PhD, whose scientific background and established network across the European pharmaceutical and biotech industry makes her uniquely placed to deliver at this level.
