Our Mission

Advocacy Over Agency™

Our mission is supporting yours. Thoughtfully, intelligently and with genuine advocacy.

Meaningful career decisions and critical hires deserve careful representation. They require time, perspective and a genuine understanding of the people involved.

VoltConsulting was built with that principle in mind. As an independent firm owned and led by partners personally invested in the work we do, we approach every engagement with care, attention and long-term perspective.

We take the time to understand the ambitions behind each role and every career move. Through listening, insight and considered advocacy, we bring the right individuals into conversation and help those conversations lead somewhere meaningful.

When our clients and candidates move closer to their mission, ours has been fulfilled.
