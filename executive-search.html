Swiss Biopharma · Oncology

Multiple Senior Functional Heads & Scientists - Oncology & Immuno-Oncology

Engaged by an organically growing Swiss biopharma to hire senior functional heads and scientists with Oncology and Immuno-Oncology experience. Worked closely with the CMO, CSO and leadership team following detailed strategic briefings.

Swiss Pharma · 10+ Year Partnership

Preferred Supplier - Long-Term Talent Partnership

A long-standing collaboration with the HR function, CSO and departmental heads at a major Swiss pharmaceutical company, operating as a consultative talent partner across multiple functions over more than a decade.

Successfully Placed: Senior Specialists (10+ years' experience) including Toxicologists & ToxPathologists, DMPK Scientists, PK/PD Modelling & Simulation Experts, and Global Medical Publications Managers. Several hires have since progressed internally to lead their respective functions.
